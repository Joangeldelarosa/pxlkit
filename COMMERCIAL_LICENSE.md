## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can my end users use the icons?**
Yes. End users of your application do not need their own license. Your license covers the application and all its users.

**I bought Indie but need Team. Can I upgrade?**
Yes! Contact info@pxlkit.xyz and we'll credit your Indie payment toward the Team license.

**Do I need a license per developer?**
No. Licenses are per-project (Indie) or per-organization (Team), not per-developer.

**What if my project has multiple domains?**
If the domains serve the same product (e.g., app.example.com and www.example.com), it counts as one project. Different products with different domains require separate licenses or a Team license.

**Do contributors get a free license?**
Yes! Contributors who have icons, code, or significant documentation merged into pxlkit receive a complimentary Team license.
